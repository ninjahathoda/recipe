Homemade Bagels

Ingredients

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon barley malt syrup (optional)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Sesame seeds, poppy seeds, or other toppings (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine bread flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and barley malt syrup (if using) to the dry ingredients. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions and shape each portion into a ball.
  5. Poke a hole in the center of each dough ball and stretch it into a bagel shape. Make the hole slightly larger than you want the finished bagel to be, as it will shrink during boiling and baking.
  6. Place the shaped bagels on a lightly greased baking sheet, cover them with a clean kitchen towel, and let them rise for about 20-30 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add the barley malt syrup (if using).
  8. Boil the bagels, a few at a time, for about 1-2 minutes per side. Use a slotted spoon to remove them from the water and place them back on the baking sheet.
  9. Brush the boiled bagels with beaten egg wash and sprinkle with sesame seeds, poppy seeds, or other desired toppings.
  10. Bake the bagels for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown and firm to the touch.
  11. Remove from the oven and let cool on a wire rack before serving.

Enjoy your Homemade Bagels!
