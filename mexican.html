Mexican Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup refried beans
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapenos (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread refried beans evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Spread salsa over the refried beans.
  5. Sprinkle shredded Mexican cheese blend over the salsa.
  6. Top with sliced black olives, diced bell peppers, diced onions, and sliced jalapenos, if using.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing.
  9. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Mexican Pizza!

Enjoy your pizza!
