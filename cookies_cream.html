Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 10-12 chocolate sandwich cookies, crushed

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  3. Stir in crushed chocolate sandwich cookies.
  4. Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  5. Scoop and enjoy your delicious cookies and cream ice cream!

Enjoy your ice cream!
