Snickerdoodle Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together softened butter and 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add dry ingredients to the creamed mixture and mix until dough forms.
  5. In a small bowl, combine 3 tablespoons granulated sugar and ground cinnamon.
  6. Shape dough into balls and roll them in the cinnamon-sugar mixture to coat evenly.
  7. Place dough balls on prepared baking sheets, spacing them apart.
  8. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cookies are set and edges are lightly golden.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  10. Enjoy your delicious homemade snickerdoodle cookies!
