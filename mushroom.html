Mushroom Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms (button or cremini)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sliced mushrooms. Cook until mushrooms are tender and any liquid has evaporated.
  3. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Top with cooked mushrooms and garlic.
  7. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing.
  10. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Mushroom Pizza!

Enjoy your pizza!
