Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until well blended.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the creamed mixture and mix well.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons onto prepared baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  10. Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies!
