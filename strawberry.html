Strawberry Cake

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup diced strawberries

For the Strawberry Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup mashed strawberries
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

For the Cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add to the creamed mixture alternately with milk, beating well after each addition.
  5. Fold in diced strawberries.
  6. Pour batter into prepared cake pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove from oven and let cool in pans for 10 minutes. Then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

For the Strawberry Frosting:

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat butter until creamy.
  2. Add powdered sugar, mashed strawberries, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat until smooth and creamy.

Assembly

  1. Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of strawberry frosting on top.
  2. Repeat with remaining cake layer and frosting.
  3. Optional: Garnish with sliced strawberries on top.

Enjoy your cake!
