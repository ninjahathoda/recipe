Brioche Bread

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 75g granulated sugar
  • 5 large eggs, beaten
  • 150ml warm milk
  • 150g unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, yeast, sugar, beaten eggs, and warm milk. Mix until a dough forms.
  2. Add the softened butter and salt to the dough. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  4. Punch down the dough and divide it into equal portions. Shape each portion into a ball or desired shape and place them in a greased baking pan or brioche molds.
  5. Cover the dough again with a kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Brush the risen dough with beaten egg wash for a shiny finish (optional).
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown and the bread sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool on a wire rack before serving.

Enjoy your Brioche Bread!
