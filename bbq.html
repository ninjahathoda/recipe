BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread BBQ sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Evenly distribute shredded chicken over the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the chicken.
  6. Top with thinly sliced red onion.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing.
  9. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious BBQ Chicken Pizza!

Enjoy your pizza!
