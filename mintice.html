Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • Green food coloring (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and peppermint extract until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. If desired, add a few drops of green food coloring to achieve a minty green color.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  4. Stir in mini chocolate chips.
  5. Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  6. Scoop and enjoy your delicious mint chocolate chip ice cream!

Enjoy your ice cream!
