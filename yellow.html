Boxed Yellow Cake with Chocolate Frosting

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 box yellow cake mix
  • Ingredients listed on the cake mix box (eggs, oil, water)

For the Chocolate Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

For the Cake:

  1. Follow the instructions on the boxed cake mix to prepare the cake batter.
  2. Preheat oven to the temperature specified on the cake mix box.
  3. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  4. Divide the cake batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
  5. Bake according to the instructions on the cake mix box, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Remove from oven and let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

For the Chocolate Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, beat butter until creamy.
  2. Add powdered sugar, cocoa powder, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat until smooth and creamy.

Assembly

  1. Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of chocolate frosting on top.
  2. Repeat with the remaining cake layer and frosting.
  3. Frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining frosting.
  4. Optional: Decorate with chocolate shavings or sprinkles.

Enjoy your cake!
