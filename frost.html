1234 Cake with Chocolate Frosting

Ingredients

For the 1234 Cake:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

For the 1234 Cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add to the creamed mixture, alternating with milk. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Divide batter evenly between prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove cakes from oven and let cool in pans for 10 minutes. Then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

For the Chocolate Frosting:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat butter until creamy.
  2. Add powdered sugar, cocoa powder, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat until smooth and creamy.
  3. If frosting is too thick, add more cream. If too thin, add more powdered sugar until desired consistency is reached.

Assembly

  1. Once the cakes are completely cooled, frost with the chocolate frosting.
  2. Optionally, decorate with chocolate shavings or sprinkles.
  3. Serve and enjoy your 1234 Cake with Chocolate Frosting!

Enjoy your cake!
