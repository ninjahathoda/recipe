Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Italian seasoning
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Season with Italian seasoning and crushed red pepper flakes, if desired.
  7. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Pepperoni Pizza!

Enjoy your pizza!
