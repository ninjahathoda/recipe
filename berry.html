Berry Chantilly Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 box white cake mix (plus ingredients listed on the box)
  • 1 lb fresh mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bake the white cake according to the instructions on the box. Let it cool completely.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  3. Cut the cooled cake horizontally into two layers.
  4. Spread a layer of whipped cream on top of the bottom cake layer.
  5. Add a layer of mixed berries on top of the whipped cream.
  6. Place the second cake layer on top and cover the entire cake with the remaining whipped cream.
  7. Garnish with additional berries and mint leaves if desired.
  8. Refrigerate the cake for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Enjoy your Berry Chantilly Cake!
