Chocolate Marble Cake

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Marble Cake:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

For the Chocolate Marble Cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add to the creamed mixture, alternating with milk. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Divide the batter in half. Mix cocoa powder into one half of the batter.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of each batter alternately into the prepared pan.
  7. Using a knife, gently swirl the batters together to create a marbled effect.
  8. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Assembly

  1. Once the cake is completely cooled, slice and serve.
  2. Enjoy your delicious Chocolate Marble Cake!
